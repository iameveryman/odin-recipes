Carolina Style Pulled Pork

Description

This Slow Cooker Carolina Style pulled pork reminds me of my childhood. It's got a tangy sweet heat that goes perfectly on a nice warm bun with your favorite coleslaw and it's so easy even I can do it!

Ingredients

  • 1 (5 pound) bone-in pork shoulder
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 1 1/2 cups apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons hot pepper sauce
  • 2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
  • 2 teaspoons crushed red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Season pork shoulder with salt and pepper and place into a slow cooker. Pour vinegar around the pork and cook on low for 12 hours. Pork should easily pull apart into strands.
  2. Remove pork from the slow cooker and discard any bones. Strain off the liquid, reserving 2 cups. Shred pork using tongs or two forks, and return to the slow cooker.
  3. Stir brown sugar, hot pepper sauce, cayenne pepper, and red pepper flakes into the reserved liquid. Mix into pork in the slow cooker.
  4. Cover and keep on Low or Keep Warm setting until ready to serve.
